Stats started his pro gaming career during his first official StarCraft II match at the hybrid 2011-2012 Proleague Season 2. Since then he has placed consistently well in various Korean StarCraft II tournaments including WCS GSL and SSL. In 2016 he was unable to qualify in Season 1 GSL Code S after placing 25th-48th in Code A.
